Official Rules of NWA Fantasy - EURO 2016

About

This fantasy game application is for managing predictions and subsequent points for UEFA Euro Cup 2016.

Registration

Registration deadline for creating a player account is July 10th, 2016.

Prediction rules

  • Match predictions will be available no later than 12 hours before the next match (or after completion of last match of the previous day, whichever is later).
  • Match predictions will be scored in the following way on completion of the match:
    • Win - 3 points
    • Loss - 0 points
  • A draw match is considered a loss for a player unless said player has predicted a draw.
  • A player will be awarded win points if player predicts a draw and match ends in a draw.
  • Points will be awarded after the completion of the match.

Prediction Lockdown

  • Predictions for each match will be locked 15 minutes prior to the match.
  • If a player misses a prediction, he/she will forfeit the points for that match.

Entry fee

  • Entry fee is $15
  • Entry fee can be transferred to pradhanparas@gmail.com if player has a Bank of America account. If not, please contact the admin and arrangements can be made.

  • If multiple players are contending for any of the above places, the following rules will apply:
    • If multiple players are tied in the 1st position, a coin toss between the players will decide the winner and subsequent position of the other players.
    • All remaining positions will be decided with a coin toss in a similar manner, after prioritizing players that were pushed out from previous higher level wins
    • As an example, if A, B, C, D and E are playing, and in the final scores A and B are tied for 1st position, C and D are tied in 2nd position, and E is in 3rd position, the final prize distribution will be decided as follows:
      • Toss between A and B > A wins > A gets 1st prize, B gets 2nd prize
      • Toss between C and D >D wins > D gets 3rd prize
